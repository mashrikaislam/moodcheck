About Vibe Check

A mood-based recommendation engine for books and music.

What it does

Vibe Check takes a free-text description of how you're feeling and matches it to a real book and song. -- !!

Why I built it

I read a lot and listen to a lot of music, and I noticed both are usually mood-driven for me — I pick a book or a song based on how I feel that day, not just what's popular. I wanted to see if I could build something that captured that instinct.

How it works

  1. Mood extraction — an LLM call converts your free-text mood into structured emotional tags
  2. Book search — those tags query Open Library's real catalog for matching subjects
  3. Ranking — books are scored by how many tags overlap with their subjects
  4. Explanation — a second LLM call explains why the top-ranked book fits your mood, and suggests a matching song
  5. Persistence — every match is saved to a real database so your history sticks around

Built with
